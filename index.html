Brief Bio:

Dr Yitian Zhao is currently the Vice Director and professor of Institute of Biomedical Engineering, Ningbo Institute of Materials Technology and Engineering, Chinese Academy of ScienceLab of Intelligent Medical Imaging (iMED). His research expertise is ophthalmic medical image processing, vessel structure analysis, eye and multi-organ joint computing and analyzing. He is also serving as an Associate Editor of IEEE Trans. Medical Imaging, Medical Physics, and Frontiers in Medicine, Area Chair of MICCAI 2021-2023. He has a contract of honorary lecturer with the Department of Eye and Vision Science, University of Liverpool. He received his BEng, MSc in 2008 and 2009 in UK, respectively. He finished his PhD degree in 3D Image Analysis from Department of Computer Science at Aberystwyth University in 2013. Previously, he was a lecturer at the Beijing Institute of Technology from 2015 to 2017, and worked as a postdoctoral and lecturer in the Department of Eye and Vision Science at University of Liverpool since 2013 to 2015.
